Who we are?

CRYSTAL MARKETING CENTRE is a world-class organisation, built on the tenets and principles of corporate ethics and excellence, to provide total marketing support for organisational and individual needs in training, recruitment and consultancy. CRYSTAL was established to play a major role in corporate and entrepreneurial development and support by advancing the importance of marketing, not only as a career, but also as the way to orientate a business.

We are equipped to serve:

  • Professionals who need to improve their skills to deliver current, relevant and value-service to their employers.

  • Thousands of fresh graduates who are not employable except they get the right skills to meet the needs of today’s organisations.

  • Over 5,000 companies in corporate Nigeria who desire world-class professional marketing services for profitable and sustainable growth.
